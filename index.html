Some Relativity Applets

Here are some Javascript applets that try to illustrate some effects of Special Relativity. Be warned that this is a work in progress and any of them might change at any time.

  • An illustration of simultaneity, using the classic "flashbulb in the middle of a train car" approach.
  • By finding the difference in arrival time of a signal from an event at two sensors, you may restrict the event to lie on a hyperbola.
  • If you have four such sensors, you may (over-)specify the event location.
  • In 2+1D, you need only three such sensors to determine when and where an event happened. Here, two detectors do this, but in different reference frames.
  • We may draw the intersection hyperbolas from each pair of sensors, and discover that it tells us nothing new.
  • An applet with two ships that happens to demonstrate the Doppler effect.
